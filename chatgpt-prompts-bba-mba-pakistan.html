ChatGPT prompts for Marketing assignments

Marketing · BBA Level

4Ps of Marketing — Pakistan brand analysis

You are an expert academic writer helping a BBA student at a Pakistani university.

Task: Write a 1000-word assignment analyzing the 4Ps of Marketing (Product, Price, Place, Promotion) for a Pakistani brand of my choice.
Brand: Daraz Pakistan
Subject: Principles of Marketing
Education Level: BBA (2nd year)
Language: Academic English, formal tone
Structure: Introduction, one section per P with heading, Conclusion
Instructions: Use real Pakistan market data and examples. Do not use Western brands as examples.

Marketing · MBA Level

Consumer buying behavior in Pakistan

You are an expert academic writer for MBA level students in Pakistan.

Task: Write a 1500-word research-based assignment on consumer buying behavior in the Pakistani market.
Subject: Consumer Behavior (MBA)
Focus: How cultural, social, and economic factors unique to Pakistan influence buying decisions
Include: Real examples from Pakistani consumers, e-commerce trends in Pakistan, and reference to local brands
Language: Formal academic English
Structure: Abstract, Introduction, 3 main sections with headings, Conclusion, References (suggest 3 real sources)

Subject: Financial Management (BBA)
