How top university assignments are different

A generic "write my assignment" prompt works for a descriptive 500-word submission. It does not work for a LUMS MBA case study or an IBA economics paper. The difference is that top university assignments expect you to argue a position, support it with evidence, acknowledge counterarguments, and write with academic precision.

❌ What does not work at LUMS, IBA, NUST, FAST Descriptive prompts that ask ChatGPT to "explain" or "describe" a topic. Top universities want analysis, argument, and critical evaluation — not summaries.
✅ What works at top Pakistani universities Prompts that specify "critically analyze," ask for "evidence-based arguments," request "APA citations," and mention the exact academic level. Every prompt below is written this way.

Prompts for LUMS students

LUMS · Business / Social Sciences

Critical essay — economics or policy topic

You are an expert academic writer for an undergraduate honors student at a top Pakistani university (equivalent to LUMS standard).

Task: Write a critical essay on the following topic: [Insert your topic]
Subject: [Insert subject — e.g. Development Economics / Public Policy / Organizational Behavior]
Word count: 1500 words
Standard: Honors undergraduate — high critical thinking, evidence-based arguments, nuanced analysis
Structure: Introduction with clear thesis, 3 analytical body paragraphs each making a distinct argument, Counterargument paragraph, Conclusion that synthesizes not summarizes
Citations: Suggest 4 real academic sources in APA format at the end
Pakistan context: Where relevant, ground the argument in Pakistan's specific situation
Tone: Formal academic English, analytical — not descriptive

LUMS · MBA

Strategic management analysis

You are a senior academic writing assistant for an MBA student at a top-tier Pakistani business school.

Task: Write a strategic management analysis on [Insert company or industry]
Framework to use: Porter's Five Forces + VRIO analysis
Word count: 1800 words
Level: MBA — requires sophisticated business reasoning, not surface-level description
Pakistan context: Analyze within Pakistan's competitive market environment
Structure: Executive summary, Porter's Five Forces analysis with Pakistan-specific competitive dynamics, VRIO resource analysis, Strategic recommendations, Conclusion
Citations: Suggest 3-4 credible business or academic sources
Tone: MBA-level analytical English — precise, evidence-referenced, professionally argued

Prompts for IBA Karachi students

IBA Karachi · BBA / MBA

Marketing or HRM case analysis

You are an academic writing assistant for a student at IBA Karachi — one of Pakistan's most rigorous business schools.

Task: Write a case analysis on the following: [Insert case topic or company]
Subject: [Marketing / HRM / Finance / Strategy]
Word count: 1200 words

Structure: Situation Analysis, Problem Statement, Analysis using relevant theory or framework, Recommendations, Conclusion
Pakistan business context: Use Karachi / Pakistan corporate sector references where applicable
Tone: Professional business English — clear, concise, and analytically strong
Citations: APA format, suggest 3 real sources

Universal prompts for any top Pakistani university

Any Top University · Research Paper

Research paper outline and draft

You are an expert academic writer for a student at a top Pakistani university.

Task: Write a research paper on: [Insert topic]
Subject: [Insert subject]
Word count: 2000 words
Level: [Undergraduate Honors / MBA / MS — choose one]

Pakistan relevance: Ground the research question and analysis in Pakistan's context
Citations: APA 7th edition — suggest 5-6 real academic sources
Tone: Formal scholarly English — rigorous, precise, and analytically strong
Important: Do not just describe the topic — argue a position and support it with reasoning

Frequently asked questions

Do LUMS and IBA students use ChatGPT for assignments? +
Yes. Students at LUMS, IBA, NUST, FAST and other top Pakistani universities use ChatGPT regularly. The difference is they need more rigorous output — which requires better prompts than what most students write.
Does LUMS or IBA have a policy on using ChatGPT? +
Both LUMS and IBA have academic integrity policies. Submitting AI-generated text as your own is a violation. Use ChatGPT for structuring, drafting, and ideas — then rewrite in your own words. Always check your course syllabus for specific AI use guidelines.
What type of assignments do NUST and FAST students use ChatGPT for? +
NUST and FAST students use ChatGPT for technical reports, lab report writeups, programming concept explanations, research paper outlines, and presentation preparation. The prompts in this guide cover all of these.
How is a ChatGPT prompt for LUMS different from a regular university prompt? +
LUMS and IBA assignments demand higher critical thinking, proper citations, and well-argued positions. Your prompt needs to specify honors or MBA level, ask for critical analysis not just description, and request APA or Harvard citations. All prompts in this guide are built to that standard.
Can I use these prompts for other Pakistani universities too? +
Absolutely. These prompts work for any Pakistani university — Bahria, CBM, UET, Punjab University, Karachi University, and others. Just adjust the education level in the prompt to match your program. The Pakistan context built into every prompt applies everywhere.
