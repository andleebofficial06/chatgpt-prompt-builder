Frequently Asked Questions
Is this tool completely free? +
Yes, 100% free. No login, no signup, no hidden charges. Just fill the form and get your prompt instantly.
Do I need a ChatGPT account to use this? +
You need a ChatGPT account to use the generated prompt, but this builder itself requires nothing. Copy the prompt and paste it directly into ChatGPT.
Will my teacher know I used AI? +
This tool helps you write better prompts so ChatGPT gives more accurate, structured answers. Always follow your institution's academic integrity policy and use AI responsibly.
Can I use this for Urdu medium assignments? +
Yes. Select "Easy Urdu-English Mix" as your language style and the prompt will instruct ChatGPT to respond in a mix that's easier to understand.
Which universities is this tool suitable for? +
This tool works for students from any Pakistani university — LUMS, IBA, FAST, NUST, UET, Punjab University, Karachi University, and all other institutions.
How is this different from just typing in ChatGPT directly? +
Most students type vague one-line questions and get generic answers. This builder structures your prompt with education level, task type, format, language style and teacher instructions — so ChatGPT gives you a response that's actually usable for your assignment.
